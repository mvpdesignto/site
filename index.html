Profile

MVP Design is a distinctive consulting studio specializing in contemporary home design that combines luxury and sustainability. The founder Mike Wesley, is an architect with award-winning experience in planning and design completing high quality green-luxury projects internationally. The studio with remote locations in Toronto and Tiny, Ontario, maximizes the vision, planning, and design (MVPD) of projects creating sustainable design solutions.

We collaborate with our clients to innovate unique custom projects that cater to their needs. The objective is to ensure the design process leads to constructability that is efficient, effective, and aesthetically pleasing while being net positive for the environment.

Sustainability is a core value of the studio, which is achieved by working closely with clients to ensure that their projects are not only beautiful and functional but also environmentally responsible. MVP Design believes in a holistic approach to environmental protection, planning, and architectural design.

In addition to expertise in architectural design, the studio is also experienced in real estate development, ensuring that designs are not only elegant but also economically viable. The studio is passionate about creating innovative and sustainable projects that enhance the lives of the people who use them.

Process

MVP Design stands out for its unique approach to collaborating with clients. We integrate visualization and design to create compelling projects for our clients. Our visionary studio has a holistic approach to planning, design, and sustainability. Sustainability is a core value of our firm, and we believe that it is essential to consider the impact of our work on the environment.

Our team is experienced in sustainable design, architecture, and real estate development. We enhance projects through a process of collaboration and visioning, from the big picture to the important details. We believe that the key to success is executing a strong vision, and we work closely with our clients to ensure that their goals are achieved.

At MVP Design, we are committed to creating sustainable, functional, and beautiful spaces that enhance the lives of the people who use them. Our approach to design is grounded in collaboration, and we believe that our clients are essential partners in the process of creating innovative and inspiring projects.
