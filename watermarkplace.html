Watermark Place is a remarkable master planned community that offers a new standard for urban living. The development is designed to be an integrated and pedestrian-friendly community that provides a high-quality urban experience. The community consists of a series of buildings that range from 12 to 30 storeys, each of which is architecturally significant and includes retail, commercial, and residential components.

One of the most notable features of Watermark Place is the beautiful tree-lined boulevards that connect the various buildings and public spaces. These boulevards offer a pleasant pedestrian experience, and they provide a natural and welcoming environment that encourages residents to explore and enjoy the community.

In addition to the beautiful public spaces, Watermark Place also features many urban amenities that are essential for a vibrant and thriving community. These amenities include a variety of restaurants and cafes, as well as numerous gathering places for residents to enjoy.

The development is designed to be a destination for the entire city, and it replaces a suburban shopping plaza with a modern, high-density community that integrates retail and residential components seamlessly. The integration of a large grocery store within the podium of the feature building provides residents with convenient access to essential goods and services, while also adding an important element of vibrancy to the community.
