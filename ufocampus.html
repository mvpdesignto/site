UFO Campus

Location
Frankfurt, Germany

Year
2015

Size 
1,500,000 sf

Project Type
Office Campus

Services
Master Planning, Campus Planning, Schematic Design Drawings, 3D Design and Visualization

Client
REIT Confidential

The UFO research campus masterplan is a unique project that will bring together several buildings for office space and research. The campus includes several buildings with different shapes and sizes, with the most notable being a round UFO-shaped tapering building and another with a sloping form and green roof. The buildings will be connected by an expansive urban plaza space, which will serve as the central hub of the development.

The plaza space will be equipped with unique street furniture that promotes pedestrian lighting and gathering, including curved linear benches. The interior of the UFO-shaped building features purple columns that provide an expansive conference level, promoting collaboration and visual interest.

The campus is designed to create a space that complements the local university and provides incubation space to support the development of innovative technologies. The project is an important addition to the community, providing a space for researchers and innovators to collaborate and explore new ideas.

The design of the campus is futuristic and unique, reflecting the nature of the research being conducted. The development will create a landmark for the community, and will undoubtedly attract attention from all over the world.
