Twisty Palace Tower is an exclusive 44-story high-rise tower that is both elegant and unique. The tower has a twisting form that gives the building its name and stands out in the skyline of the city. The clear transparent podium at the base of the building features a diagonal truss structure that gives the building a dynamic and modern feel. The space is used for events and conference spaces, which adds to the building's appeal.

The Twisty Palace Tower features boutique elevator lofts living spaces, with one unit per level up to level 34 and double-level units on the top 8 floors. Each unit is designed to be spacious and luxurious, with units on the lower levels being approximately 8,000 square feet and units on the upper levels being over 16,000 square feet. 

The elevator core incorporates a lift to move exotic cars up the building, and each unit features a garage space for several cars.

One of the most exciting features of the Twisty Palace Tower is the large balcony that comes with each unit. The balcony offers panoramic views of both the city and the bay and is a perfect place to relax and enjoy the stunning views. Overall, the Twisty Palace Tower is an exceptional example of modern architecture and design, with luxurious living spaces and breathtaking views.
