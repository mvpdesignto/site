Poke Lofts is a proposed 9 storey condo building located in the heart of a vibrant downtown area. The building is designed to seamlessly blend in with the surrounding urban context, matching the height of the street wall and incorporating modern design elements that reflect the dynamic nature of the area.

The units in Poke Lofts feature large private terraces and expansive glass walls that offer breathtaking views of the city skyline. The glazing is a hybrid curtain wall system that is both energy-efficient and maximizes views with a minimalist profile, creating a seamless flow between indoor and outdoor spaces. The penthouse suite is a true standout, featuring an infinity pool and a private sunbathing area that takes advantage of the stunning views.

One of the unique features of Poke Lofts is the communal exercise room on the second floor, which overlooks the bustling street below. This space provides residents with a convenient and inspiring space to stay active and healthy.

Overall, Poke Lofts is a beautiful and modern addition to the downtown area, offering residents a luxurious and comfortable living space with stunning views of the city. The building is designed to reflect the dynamic nature of the area and provide a true urban living experience that is unmatched in the region.
